Behind the Lens & The Trail

Meet Xander Hayes, the creator of GearInsights.

Hello. I’m Xander Hayes. Most days, you’ll find me somewhere between a rugged mountain pass and a quiet valley, carrying 40 pounds of gear and a camera strapped to my chest.

I started GearInsights because I was tired of "sanitized" reviews. In an era of sponsored content, it’s hard to tell which backpack will actually survive a week of torrential rain or which camera is truly light enough for a 20-mile day.

"My philosophy is simple: Gear should empower your journey, not hinder it. If I wouldn't trust it with my life in the backcountry, I won't recommend it to you."

With a background in technical photography and a lifetime of outdoor exploration, I blend raw field testing with analytical data. Whether it's the stitching on a pair of Garmont boots or the dynamic range of a new Sony sensor, I break down the specs that actually matter when you're off the grid.
